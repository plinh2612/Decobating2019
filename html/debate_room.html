asian parliamentary
debate format
AP format, also known as Asian Parliamentary, is the format usually used in competitions for Asian high schools and universities. Each round consistes of two teams: the supporting team (Affirmative - Government) and the opposing team (Negative - Opposition), each team has 3 members.
Team Government (abbreviated as Gov) supports the motion, with three positions: Prime Minister (PM), Debuty Prime Minister (DPM) and Government Whip (GW). Team Opposition (abbreviated as Opp) objects to the motion, with three following positions: Leader of Opposition (LO), Debuty Leader of Opposition (DLO), and Opposition Whip (OW)
